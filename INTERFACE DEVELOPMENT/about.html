About Us

Our Amazing Services

Mustang Cafe

Established in 2020, Mustang Cafe is a locally-owned, independent cafe service.we’re still happily serving the people of our community with quality fresh food. Our cafe service has grown to become one of Kathmandu’s largest and most recognized services. It’s almost as popular as our famous Yak Steak!

Our Belief

Our customers have sent us months of unforgettable experiences serving the Kathmandu city, and we think the least we can do is continue to serve them tasty dishes with a sincere smile. Customer care is a pillar of our industry, and we aspire to receive a smile in return – we hope that our food will brighten everyone's day just a little bit.

Our Quality

We take pride in the consistency and freshness of our food at Cafe Mustang. Every day, our chefs prepare everything in-house using only the finest ingredients. Furthermore, we provide our customers with mountain goods like "Yak Meat","Mountain Potatoes", etc. that make them feel the most alive.
